About Cyberbullying

Cyberbullying is the use of electronic communication to bully or harass individuals. It can cause lasting harm, affecting mental health and self-esteem.

Our mission is to raise awareness, educate, and empower individuals to take a stand against online harassment.

The Impact of Cyberbullying

59%

of teens have experienced some form of online harassment.

37%

of children report long-term mental health effects from cyberbullying.

80%

of bystanders choose not to intervene.

How You Can Help

Be a Supportive Friend

Listen and encourage those affected by cyberbullying to seek help.

Report Abuse

Use reporting features on platforms to flag inappropriate content.

Spread Awareness

Share information and encourage open conversations about online safety.
