What Is Cyberbullying?

Cyberbullying refers to the use of digital platforms to harass, intimidate, or harm others. Unlike traditional bullying, cyberbullying takes place online, allowing the perpetrator to target individuals anonymously or publicly. It occurs across various platforms, including social media, messaging apps, gaming communities, and email..

We aim to raise awareness, educate, and empower individuals to stand against online harassment.
